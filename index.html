I am a chemist with a background in both experimental and computational research (USA and Canada) coupled with extensive teaching experience. I have acquired valuable experience as a Chemical adviser and REACH registration manager (Slovenia) .

My skill set includes three years working as a Chemical adviser and REACH registration manager at the fitopharmaceutical industry PINUS TKI d.d and biodiesel company BioGoriva d.o.o., Slovenia. My chemical adviser specialities include managment of: Plant Protection Product regulations (label authorization), Biocides, REACH registration managment, GHS/CLP, MSDS, PIC, Dangerous good (DG) transport.

I have approx 7 years of combined experience in physical chemistry research (Saint Mary's University (SMU), Canada), and biophysical chemistry (University of South Florida (USF), USA).

My targeted areas of research at USF have been centered on projects guided toward the improvement of public health (Alzheimer’s disease, Parkinson’s disease and cancer). My chemistry master’s research at SMU targeted improving safety of Generation IV Super-Critical Water Cooled nuclear reactor by understanding its water chemistry.
